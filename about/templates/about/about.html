About

>

Hi. My name is Chris and I am the artist behind Theoretics Art.

Based in Wales, I began exploring art over the first lockdown in 2020. I was interested in working with bold colours and shapes and from this curiosity, Theoretics Art was created.

Theoretics Art paintings flirt with abstraction and street art with essences from multiple abstract art styles.Every original artwork I create, I aim to make a statement piece that brings excitement and personality into the room.

Acrylic and spray paint are my core mediums. I work with layering, building on top of layers to create a depth to the pieces that allow observers to find something new every time. The focal point of each piece brings a sense of reason and focus that draws eyes to the piece, and the viewers experience begins.

I'm an avid walker and like to get out into the mountains frequently. Inspired by the Welsh hills, I began to teach myself photography - if only to capture those fleeting moments of dry, clear weather whilst hiking the Brecon Beacons! This interest soon developed into a secondary creative outlet. My photography is inspired by the beauty of nature and how humans live and interact in the world.
