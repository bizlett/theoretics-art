FAQs

How can I contact you?

If you need to reach out to me, you can contact me directly on info@theoreticsart.com

Do you accept commissioned projects?

Yes, absolutely!

  1. Contact Theoretics Art on info@theoreticsart.com. We will have an informal chat by phone/email about what you might like. If you aren’t sure we can work with complementing colour schemes for the room where this unique artwork will be.
  2. Once the commission has been confirmed I will keep you up to date on progress (it’s a collaboration after all): This can include pictures and video updates!
  3. When the painting is completed I will package it up carefully. It will be delivered to you pre-wired, ready to hang.
  4. Unwrap the painting, place it on your wall and revel in your one of a kind painting!

Do you have a newsletter I can sign up to?

At the moment I don't have a newsletter. But you can follow the latest Theoretics Art news via my social media.

Do you offer instalment payment plans?

At the moment, I can only accept payment in full for artwork purchased.

Do you have a return policy?

I do not operate a standard returns policy due to the individual nature of each piece I create. If there is an issue with your artwork, please contact me directly. I'm happy to discuss how we might resolve any problems on a case by case basis.

Do you rent or lease your artwork and what are your fees?

On a case by case basis I may be able to rent or lease my artwork. If you are interested in renting or leasing any of my original artwork, please contact me directly to discuss!

Does your original artwork come with a certificate of authenticity?

Yes. All my original paintings come with a certificate of authenticity to verify that they are a Theoretics Art original painting.

Do you help customers with selecting frames for your artwork?

Framing isn't a service I offer but if you're after general advice about framing your Theoretics Artwork, feel free to reach out to me on info@theoreticsart.com

How long will it take for my Theoretics Art artwork to arrive?

Please allow 4-7 days for your artwork to arrive. This allows me the time to properly process your order and ensure it's packaged correctly so it can arrive safely to you.

Where do you ship your artwork?

At the moment, I can only accept orders for UK delivery.

How much is shipping?

Shipping is calculated at 10% of the orders total. This is because shipping can vary according to the size of the artwork.
